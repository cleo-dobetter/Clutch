How to Play Clutch

  • The Setup: The game uses a standard deck + 2 Jokers. Each player gets a 20-card "personal deck" and draws a 5-card hand.
  • The Goal: Be the first player to empty both your hand and your personal deck!
  • Playing Cards: You must play a card on the center pile that matches the Suit OR is exactly One Rank Up/Down from the top card. (Kings wrap around to Aces).
  • Wilds: Jokers are Wild and can be played on anything, and anything can be played on them.
  • Your Turn:
    • If you have fewer than 5 cards in your hand, you automatically draw from your personal deck.
    • Play a valid card. If you cannot play, you must draw from the Center Pile.
    • If you still cannot play after drawing, your turn passes.
  • Straight Clutch Mode: If you ever have a 5-card Straight in your hand at any point, you instantly win the game! Jokers can act as any card in the straight, and straights can wrap around (e.g., Q-K-A-2-3).
